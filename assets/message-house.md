## Rules for this document

- **3–5 pillars maximum.** More than 5 means you haven't decided.
- **Every pillar earns its slot** with a one-line claim AND proof. No proof = no pillar.
- **Tag every candidate message:**
  - **must-say** — the keynote fails without it.
  - **should-prove** — strong support if time permits.
  - **parking-lot** — capture for other channels (blog, press release, sales) but not the stage.
- **The roof is one sentence.** If it's two, you have two ideas.
