## Column definitions
- **# / Time / Duration** — running time from show open.
- **Beat name** — short label everyone uses ("Cold open," "Customer 1," "Demo," "Vision close").
- **Speaker** — who is on stage / on camera.
- **Mode** — Live / Pre-recorded / Demo / Video / Hybrid.
- **Content** — what happens in this beat, in 1–2 lines. Not the script.
- **Asset(s)** — slide range, video filename, demo state, music cue, lower-third copy.
- **S.T.A.R.?** — is this engineered to be memorable? At least one must be ☑.
- **Owner** — single name accountable for this beat being ready.
- **Status** — Draft / In review / Approved / Locked.

## Self-check before lock
- ☐ Total duration matches runtime target ±5%
- ☐ Every beat has a named owner
- ☐ At least one ☑ S.T.A.R. moment
- ☐ No flat stretch > 3 minutes without tension shift
- ☐ Every speaker transition has a clear handoff
- ☐ Every demo and video has a backup plan referenced
